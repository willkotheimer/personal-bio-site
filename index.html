Background and interest in development:

When I started out, I wanted to break into writing. I enjoyed the expression and community around it. I ended up in writing camp by my Junior year of high school. But then I pivoted; I decided I wanted to make movies, and worked on a documentary film for two years. I chased that dream for a number of years, along with doing lots of side jobs to pay rent, but then decided I wanted a career and a family. About 10 years ago, I started back to school at WKU and in 2014 graduated with a Computer Science degree. Six years ago I moved to Nashville, got married, and have worked in a number of jobs in development, culminating in my current position, working in Quality Assurance. I love my job for a number of reasons, not the least of which are the great people. I decided to go to Nashville Software School because I wanted to explore some areas of development that I just hadn't gotten the opportunity to work with before, namely .Net and React. What I hope to get out of this is the chance to work in Selenium Engineering or .Net App development.
